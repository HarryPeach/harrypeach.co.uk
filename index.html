Some of my projects

Ciph

Ciph is an android app to generate secure and memorable passwords. Based on a famous xkcd comic, passwords are generated from a pool of random words making them easier to remember and providing higher entropy than a traditional password.

 Play Store  Issue Tracker

bootscreenx

A website to create boot images for the Nintendo Switch that resemble BIOS screens from early computers. Originally for the Nintendo 3DS but updated and maintained for the switch by me.

 Source Website

Skye Assistant

Originally created for my A-level coursework; Skye Assistant is a voice controlled virtual assistant. I rewrote the project with the primary goal of making it more extensible and cleaning up the codebase.
